Stay Healthy

Health Tips and Resources

  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Include a variety of foods like fruits, vegetables, grains, and proteins in your meals. Opt for homemade meals and limit intake of sugary and processed foods.
  • Stay Active: Engage in regular physical activity like walking, jogging, or cycling for at least 30 minutes a day. Exercise helps maintain weight, improves mood, and boosts overall health.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to keep your body hydrated. Limit intake of sugary drinks and opt for water, coconut water, or homemade juices.
  • Prioritize Mental Health: Take time for relaxation and stress management. Practice deep breathing, meditation, or hobbies you enjoy to reduce stress and improve mental well-being.
