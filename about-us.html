Welcome to Satyanand Hospital

At Satyanand Hospital, we are committed to providing top-quality healthcare services with compassion and excellence. Established over a decade ago in the heart of Azizganj, Shahjahanpur, Uttar Pradesh, we have been serving our community with dedication and integrity.

We are a 80-bed multi-specialty hospital equipped with modern facilities, advanced equipment, and a team of highly skilled and dedicated medical professionals. Our hospital focuses on providing high-quality care in a professional and caring environment. We are easily reachable by road, ensuring accessibility for all.

Our Story

Satyanand Hospital is more than just a healthcare facility; it's a testament to the vision and dedication of Late Shri Shukdev Mishra, whose inspiration continues to guide us every day. Founded with the aim of providing exceptional medical care to our community, our hospital has grown to become a trusted name in healthcare.

Our journey began with the vision of our esteemed patrons, Dr. Satya Prakash Mishra, Mrs. Rashmi Mishra, Dr. Anand Prakash Mishra, and Mrs. Nalini Mishra, who envisioned a hospital that would offer high-quality care with a compassionate touch. Their unwavering support and guidance have been instrumental in shaping the hospital's ethos and values.

Under the leadership of our directors, Dr. Saurabh Mishra, Mrs. Shilpi Mishra, Dr. Gaurav Mishra, and Dr. Shweta Mishra, Satyanand Hospital has flourished into a 50-bed multi-specialty facility equipped with modern amenities, specialized equipment, and a team of highly skilled medical professionals. z
